TEDxVITPune

TEDxVITPune is a platform dedicated to spreading ideas and fostering innovation among students. From selecting speakers and curating content to managing logistics, marketing, and publicity, our team embodies the spirit of innovation and collaboration. We strive to showcase groundbreaking ideas, ignite curiosity, spark meaningful conversations, and inspire action through engaging talks and events. TEDxVITPune has organised eight successful events, focusing on “ideas worth spreading” and cultivating a culture of curiosity. This year, they organised a community conversation titled Let's Talk Travel, where travel enthusiasts shared their experiences. Their upcoming event will feature speakers with over a million followers on social media as well as local artists. The event will include creative and personalised workshops, great food, enthralling performances, networking opportunities, memorable merchandise, and much more.

Our Team

At TEDxVITPune, we believe in the power of ideas to transform lives and shape the future. Within each of us lies the potential to drive change, inspire others, and leave a lasting impact on the world. TEDx is all about tapping into that potential and equipping us with the tools, inspiration, and support we need to make a difference in our community and beyond.
