Team Abhivriddhi

Abhivriddhi, VIT Punes Student Training and Development Committee since 2016, promotes student growth through educational programs. Named for upliftment, it fosters soft skills, untapped potential, and individual essence. With a commitment to excellence, it empowers students to identify interests, achieve goals, and enhance corporate readiness through expert counseling."Engineering Unplugged" highlighted insights from successful engineers, entrepreneurs, and former VITians, like Harish Uthayakumar and Shreyansh Sancheti from Bluelearn, Adit Yadav, Anushka Kulkarni (VIT alumna), and Chanakya Mandal faculty, attracting 300+ attendees. EDGE '24, led by Arsh Goyal, Dhairya Gangwani, Saurabh Bhosale, Dr. Amey Pangarkar, and Ashwini Apte, emphasizes exploration, development, and career success. Eaton Changing Gears, with Eaton industry collaboration and led by Abhivriddhi, imparts students with vital career skills through expert-led sessions, fostering personal and professional growth.

Our Team
