Team VishwaConclave

The goal of VishwaConclave 2024 was to unite students and professionals from diverse spheres of nation-building, offering them valuable perspectives and understanding on the pivotal challenges influencing Indias trajectory. The event sought to ignite passion and drive amongst students, encouraging them to strive for mastery in their respective disciplines and actively participate in fostering a thriving and robust future for India.In 2023-24, VishwaConclave organized two remarkable events: VishwaForum showcased Prof. Durgesh Tripathi and Bela Shende's celestial musical fusion, while "Ripples of Retrospect "featured Lt. Gen. Vinod Bhatia's strategic prowess, Subodh Bhave's entertainment insights, Medha Bhaskaran's historical narrative, and Mohan Savarkar's tech perspectives, complemented by engaging workshops and musical band experience.
