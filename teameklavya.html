Team Eklavya

Team Eklavya is the street play club of VIT Pune. It aims to instill a sense of responsibility and raise awareness among the citizens regarding concerning social issues. It aims to raise an awakening among the citizens against injustice and social evils like corruption, violence, etc. Providing an entertaining reflection to society and working towards a country of rationalism and awareness.Below listed are the achievements in various street play competitions (Competition name - Award - Organizing Institute). 1. "Halla Bol" - Ist Rank - IIT Guwahati 2. "Takht-e-Tamasha" - Ist Rank - Symbiosis Center for Information Technology, Pune. 3. "Nukkad Natak" - Ist Rank, Best Direction, Best Acting, Best writing - Cummins College, Pune. 4. "Suno Suno" - Ist Rank - COEP Pune. 5. "Awaz-e-Awam" - Ist Rank - IIIT Pune.

Our Team

We here at Team Eklavya fervently believe in the cultivation of moral ethics in team members and students while building a noble and thoughtful citizen. We encourage students to focus on holistic development and acquiring valuable skills that will benefit them and the society they live in.
