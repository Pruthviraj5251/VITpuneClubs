Team VishwaExpressionHub

Vishwakarma Expression Hub is the official art club of VIT, Pune. We aim to bring all art forms under one umbrella. We cater to various domains like Literature, Aesthetics, Graphic Designing, Videography, and Video Editing. We're committed to fostering a dynamic community, celebrating creativity, and enriching and inspiring artistic pursuits.VEH has orchestrated diverse campaigns and events spanning various art domains. In the notable literature event "Kavya Vaibhav" of 2023-24, featuring Vaibhav Joshi, attendees experienced enrichment "Community Meetups" foster engagement through dynamic workshops by club members. "Dastaan" and "Aurora - The Dawn of Creativity offer platforms for budding i artists. Additionally, "Expressions of Pride" | conducts annual interviews with LGBTQ+ activists.
