The Catalyst

Creating a team of highly effective and reliable seniors to guide freshmen to significantly enhance the college experience for newcomers. Empowering students, fostering personal and academic growth, promoting community engagement, and building support and giving back culture.The Mentorship program: Started with The Mentorship program for the Freshmen. recruited mentors from SY and TY to mentor the FY students for different subjects which included PSAP, EDI, Mathematics, Robotics etc. Conducted an Online session in collaboration with Nirmaan NGO which aimed at Mental Health Awareness among students. Conducted an offline event in collaboration with Jnana Prabodhani : Sankalp Vyakhyanmala where two distinguished speakers from two different fields to guide students regarding their respective domains.
