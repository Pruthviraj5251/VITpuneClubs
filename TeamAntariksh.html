Team Antariksh

Antariksh Club VI was formed with the sole idea of bringing together and forming a community of like-minded individuals and enthusiasts, who would come together to connect, collaborate, explore, and share their knowledge in the field of Astronomy and Space Science.VIT's Astronomy Club, Antariksh, recently hosted a series of enriching events to enhance its members' knowledge and skills. These included an astrophotography workshop, where attendees honed their celestial photography skills.We also celebrated Chandrayaan -3 Mahotsav, commemorating Indias remarkable lunar exploration feat. Engaging astronomy talks, featuring expert insights, captivated our audience, while lively star parties and captivating stargazing events fostered community engagement. The club aimed to foster scientific curiosity, learning, and camaraderie within VI. Through hands-on experiences, informative discussions, and celebratory gatherings, Antariksh united enthusiasts in their exploration of the universe's wonders.
