GDSC

The purpose of GDSC at VIT Pune is to empower students through free education in coding, fostering a culture of innovation and collaboration. It aims to equip students with essential skills for the digital era, promoting inclusivity and accessibility in tech education.GDSC conducts and manages many workshops in the technical domain some of these events are: TensorFiesta: A 3-day machine learning workshop which encompases everyting from data preprocessing to model deployment. FlutterForge: In our speaker session, industry experts illuminated the potential of Flutter and explored its capabilities and its impact on cross-platform app development. AndroidAccelerated: A session on Android development where participants learned everything from Ul design to data storage.
