Team Griffin

The purpose of Team Griffin India is to innovate and excel in the field of aviation, particularly through the development of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). With a focus on aerodynamics, avionics, manufacturing, and computer-aided engineering/design, the club aims to contribute meaningful applications of UAVs for societal benefit.Team Griffin India participated in a national drone development competition. Three teams were put in. One team won overall AIR 3 and the second team won overall AIR 6 and AIR 2 in Design Report. Recently we also conducted a drone building workshop in which students from VIT were taught how to build a drone from scratch.

Our Team

We are an aeromodelling team. We all from the team love to build and fly drones and the very same passion brings us all together. It's more of a family than a team.
